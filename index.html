About

The founders of Future Leaders Network (“FLN”) met by chance, but soon discovered they had something in common - each struggled to find a network of professionals who are on the rise and striving to be future leaders in their community of White Rock South Surrey / Surrey.

Where do the people like me gather at a time and place I want to be? Unable to find this space, the Founders decided to build it themselves.

FLN was born out of a shared vision of a space where future leaders connect, engage and collaborate. By hosting events at local businesses, FLN brings driven, innovative and influential business leaders, professionals, and entrepreneurs together. FLN's mission is to inspire, equip and enable future leaders to collaborate for the prosperity of the greater Surrey area.
